9Agent health & performance — Tier 1 cohort only

Agent health overview

"Health score" is a 0–100 composite for Tier 1 assignees with ≥30 tickets. Higher useful Tech Notes % and structured-notes %. Closer to cohort baseline reach-up rate (currently ~5%) — both "not asking enough" and "asking too often" are penalized. Lower unresolved %, "None specified" rate, technical incident tickets closed in <30 min (rushing real problems), parts tickets stale >21 days, and no-parts tickets stale >14 days.
